General Meeting 4-APR-98
Name: First General Meeting, 1st Quarter
Time: 2000 GMT-2300 GMT
Date: SAT 4-APR-98
General: RTEMS, 1Q1998, JFA
Location: www.jos.org/chat
Alternate: IRC #jos EFNET? (if down)
Schedule:
20:00 GMT General chat, pre-discussion.
20:15 GMT 1Q1998 Summary,
20:30 GMT JOS/JFA Relationship
20:45 GMT JOS Kernel - RTEMS ?
21:00 GMT Free/Open Discussion

(20:15)For further meetings:

  • Give times in UTC, CET, EST, (Clark)
  • Try and get chat logging working (Markus is already working on it)
New business (no old yet)

1Q1998: Summary report

  • New wiki, Source Server, Pretty Printer, Chat
  • Two possible kernels explored RTEMS and OSKit
  • RTEMS background explained by Clark
JFA:

  • JOS supports the JavaLobby and JFA but does not think they belong together.
  • Clark states his belief that JFA will be hurt if kept a part of JL
  • No one disagrees.
RTEMS:(20:45)

  • Kaffe is discussed briefly. It needs serious hacking before it will be useful to us.
  • Japhar is mentioned, but none present seem to know much about it.
  • Clark will email an RTEMS summary out this week
  • Japhar discussion should start on the kernel list.
Kernel/General:(2100)

  • The idea of hacking Linux to provide a development platform is explored.
  • This gives us a working platform to build off of now, so non kernel work can get started.
  • Also discussed is an RTEMS based development platform, with Linux drivers
  • JosDevelopmentPlatform is started to discuss such things.
  • It looks like JOS will be using RTEMS for it's base.
JVM:

  • There is an existing port of Kaffe to RTEMS, but it needs some work still.
  • JVM group needed, see JVMGroup
Goals for 2Q1998:

  • A bootable jvm
  • A license
  • More applications or beans (browser?)
  • support for non-awt classfiles (?)
Personal notes:

  • Clark will be a lot less accessible over the next two months as he works on a book about free software and free OSes. This will bring JOS some press when it is published, but it will keep him very busy for awhile.

Broke up about 21:45 or so.
