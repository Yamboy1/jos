To Get Started

  • Learn how to use JOS's collaborative software, called Wiki Wiki at AboutWiki
  • Read the JOS FAQ
  • Read up on GoodStyle
  • Put your self in the MemberList (In alphabetical order _please_!!!).
  • Add your skills to the Resources list.
  • Sign up on the MailingLists of interest.
  • Read some of the MailingLists archives.
  • Explore the various WorkingGroups
  • Join or start a project. Put yourself on the ProjectList.
  • See if there are VolunteersWanted.
  • Browse and contribute to the Readings and TheGlossary
  • Install some DevelopmentTools and start getting your hands ditry.
  • Help us organize the project better.
And most importantly...

  • Have Fun and Learn
