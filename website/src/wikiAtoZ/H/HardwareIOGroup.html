The HardwareIOGroup is concerned with issues relating to JOS's interaction with the hardware for IO.

Specific issues include:

  • Graphics Card
  • Video support
  • 3D support
  • Audio support
  • Serial Ports
  • Parallel Ports
  • SCSI support
  • USB support
  • IEEE 1394(FireWire) support
  • PCMCIA support
  • PCI/ISA/NuBus/whatever system bus
