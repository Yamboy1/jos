SmartAPIPages

Article contributed by GilbertHerschberger (20 September 1999, updated 27 December 2001).

Article Index

The smart object factory is a general purpose factory. A broad range of different digital resources can be retrieved from a single factory. A digital resource is identified with a uniform resource identifier. This index shows the top-level protocols and some of their related pages.
