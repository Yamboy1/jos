Notice

16 December 2001

This is a status report for what's been going on with the JOS Project homepage.
  1. Helmut let the domain name registration for jos.org expire in October, 2001. Unfortunately, he did not mention this to any other member of the JOS Project at the time. Recently, he explained that his reason for doing so was because, judging by lack of "traffic", the project "seems to have fizzled out". He also characterizes the project as a "hobby", rather than a "real project".
  2. The actual DNS entry was removed two weeks ago, taking the JOS Project homepage "off the air". This is the first indication we had that there might be a problem. Only our homepage remained on jos.org server. Other resources, such as JOS Wiki, CVS and our mailing lists were not affected. None of our sister projects were affected.
    • JOS Wiki: http://jos.sourceforge.net/
  3. Helmut has expressed his desire for a status report and outline of a plan for the JOS Project. When this was provided, he continued to express his need for a status report and a plan. What is he asking for? I have suggested that it sounds to me as if Helmut doesn't agree with what we're doing.
  4. Helmut has further expressed his desire to "recover" some of the costs of sponsoring the project. What is he asking for? I have asked him to explain how he thought this might happen.
Meanwhile, we are continuing to use our mailing lists, such as this one, to keep our members informed. We are going to set up a wiki article to keep wikizens informed as well.

Note: A copy of this status report has been forwarded to Helmut and posted on the jos-general@lists.sourceforge.net mailing list.

-- GilbertHerschberger
