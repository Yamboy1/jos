Jos Wiki

Introduction

JOS Wiki is now static. Its pages are now maintained in CVS. Pages on our website are rendered by an Ant script.

No more interactive editing

With the decision of sourceforge.net to switch of MySQL from 3.x to 4.x, it seemed like the right time to freeze this content.

User accounts

JOS Wiki is read-only in your favorite HTML browser. Access to these pages is anonymous. Therefore, it is no longer necessary to maintain user accounts, with name and password. (user.php now redirects to this article.)
