JOSDI is a sub-project of JOS whose purpose is to implement a debugger interface. I am basing my work on the Java(tm) Platform Debugger Architecture specification from Sun Microsystems. The JPDA specifies a standard for connecting debugger applications to Java(tm) virtual machines.

It is my hope that JOSDI will enhance the compatibility of JOS with other Java(tm) development environments and tools.

See http://home.earthlink.net/~jdunrue/josdi/index.html for more details.
