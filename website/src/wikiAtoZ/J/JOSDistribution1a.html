DistributionGroup; JOSDistribution1d

Article contributed by GilbertHerschberger (24 November 1999).

Current distribution

The current distribution of JOS is JOSDistribution1d. This is where you'll find the BytecodeNativeInterface and SmartAPI.

Release 1a

The earliest distribution of JOS was not a formal distribution. It is everything distributed prior to the installation of CVS and Source Server. Release 1a is a place holder for all that stuff prior to CVS.
