SkillPages

Article contributed by GilbertHerschberger (19 November 2001).

Introduction

In this article, you'll learn how to contribute another skill article to JOS Wiki.

Procedure

The following procedure illustrates how to contribute another skill article.
  1. Click the Wiki link on the JOS Project homepage.
  2. Find the SkillPages article.
  3. Click the edit link to edit the list of skill pages. An edit article page will be displayed.
  4. Add the name of your article, using an existing or new catagory. For example, articles about skills on Linux should be found under the Linux heading.
  5. Save the index page by clicking the Save button.
  6. Click on the name of your article. A create article page will be displayed.
  7. Click the create link. An edit article page will be displayed.
  8. Type "Skill" in the Summary field, DevZone in the Web field and the text of your article in the text field. The text of your article should start with a link back to the SkillPages article and a tag line. Your tag line should show when you created the article.
  9. When finished, click the Save button. Your article will be save in the DevZone section of JOS Wiki. Thanks for your contribution!
