I am hooked on the Wiki idea, work for TakeFive Software Inc. in Cupertino, CA, peter.thoeny@takefive.com , http://www.takefive.com/

Interested in Java and JOS, but not actively contributing to JOS.

I downloaded the JOS Wiki clone and installed it on our Intranet. Unfortunately you can't see it, it is behind our firewall.

I changed the layout, so it is easier to see in which web you currently are. I also added full text search with/without regular expressions. We call our Wiki clone TWiki - pronounced "Twee Kee", short form of TakeFive Wiki. If anybody is interested, I could send the source of my changes.
