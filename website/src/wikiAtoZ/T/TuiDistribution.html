The tui distribution is aimed at providing utilities for use on the command line by reimplementing GNU utilities in Java.

Update 10/98: The tui distribution is not part of the JOS source tree and is maintained offsite. KevinRaulerson is working towards integrating a whole series of newer command line utilities into JOS. Several of these are already in org.jos.app.cli and org.jos.lib.cli Please see the CommandLineGroup for further info.

__Current major version__: 0.1
