Article contributed by GilbertHerschberger (25 July 2000).

Introduction

A CVS client is part of the CVS network protocol. It can:

  • download files from a CVSService to your computer, and
  • upload files from your computer to a CVS service.

CVS is similar to FTP. It is more powerful and more complex. You run a CVS client on your machine. Anyone can download files using an anonymous CVS account, where both name and password are equal to "anonymous". Contributors upload files using a personal account with their own name and password.
