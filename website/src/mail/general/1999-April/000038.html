I'm certainly not saying that JOS should be CLI based, but rather, as I
believe has been discussed, both a GUI and CLI based system - that is, you can
choose whichever one you like and use it to configure the system without
limitations. And this pluggability would be part of what JADE is all about.

pluggable). And making that one, or one of the, in case of multiple default
CLIs (selected upon installation for example), bash:ish is what I propose.
