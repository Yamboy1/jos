> So the question is to integrate this and maybe other games in any form
> to JOS?

JOS is split into a few parts, mainly the OS, the Applications, and the
Games. The applications (Text editors for example) are not necessarily
distributed with the OS, neither are games. But yes, you can place the
game on the JOS server under the games category (assuming you know how
to use the Wiki). Consequently, the so called "Entire JOS Distribution"
will include your game!
