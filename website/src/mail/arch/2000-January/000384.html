Let's take a look at JOS architecture from a wish-list point of view.

What is it we wish to have documented?

There is a big difference between (1) having no documentation, and (2)
having great documentation we can all enthusiastically agree with. Here is
an example. I believe that JOS is more than just a project to build
jJOS/decaf.

Assertion 1. JOS is an independent operating system; it does not depend on
another operating system for its functionality. It is not MS-DOS, Linux,
OS/2, MS-Windows, nor UNIX.

Do you agree? If not, why not?
