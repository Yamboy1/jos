A monthly JOS Meeting will be held on the first sunday of every month on the OpenProjects IRC Network, in the #jos channel. Everyone is invited to join in. The channel is hidden so just join #jos ... it won't be listed!

NOTICE For a few months, we're going to have meetings twice a month or more often to work on some organization issues. In addition, feel free to create and hold meetings at other times just to chat or to discuss development issues.

Agenda

  • JVM/Kernel development
  • Open discussion
If you have other items you'd like to see discussed at the meeting, please add them to the agenda.

Logs of previous meetings is available at http://www.metamech.com/wiki/view/Main/Logs

These are the times for the meeting in different time zones. Please add your time zone information if it's not listed? DST is Daylight Saving Time.
