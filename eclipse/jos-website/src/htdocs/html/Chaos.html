Chaos

Chaos - War of Wizards was originally released on the ZX Spectrum back in the mid '80's. You and upto 7 others played wizards with a selection of spells, the idea being to kill your opponents. The winner was the last one standing.

It all operated on a turn based system, where each player would first select their spell, then cast it and finally then move themselves and any summomned creatures.

Chaos is one of my all time favourite games, so I thought I'd do a java conversion of it for JOS.

Project Status

Just started really. Have started writting the main class and added some helper classes. Using BoardLib for some functionality. Basically this is an excuse to test and flesh out BoardLib which I am also working on.

I could really use someone who is good at graphics to get in touch at some point. There's no immediate rush, but I'm an appalling artist and would like some nice graphics at some point.
