JOS Agenda

This page will contain the Agendas for the IRC meetings on Sundays. You can look at the following page for the OldAgendas. Please put your items to discuss on the NextMeetingAgenda page. This page is to allow us to think about a limited set of items and to discuss them in their entirety at the next meeting and hopefully deal with them once and for all.

-- IainLowe (10-02-99)

Topics
  • Discuss integrating Servlets with Apache.
    • Where can we put the Apache server?
    • Who will be responsable for it?
    • Can we run Servlets with it? (c.f. ServletsWithApache)
    • Do we need Apache for other applications or just for Servlets?
  • Discuss mailing list archiving.
    • Who will be responsable for maintaining the archive?
    • Where will we keep the archive?
  • Discuss the CVS server.
    • Who will maintain it?
    • Where will it be?
    • Who can access the source code?
  • (If we have time) Discuss membership and voting.
