This group is developing Games to promote JOS.

It is known that any OS without games is doomed, so we will try to prove that Java, yes, can be a language for developing games!

Here is what we are working on:

  • GamesJOSColony
  • GamesXMLAdventureFramework
  • GamesGameTesters
  • GamesCardLibrary (added 2/18/99 by MaxBerger)
  • GamesPaintingPipe (added 15 Nov 1999 by MattAlbrecht)
  • GamesNetPlay (added 17 Nov 1999 by MattAlbrecht)
Note: I named all these things with the prefix "Games" so they won't mix with anything else in the Wiki web. Please keep this prefix in all Wiki pages having to do with the GameGroup working group.
