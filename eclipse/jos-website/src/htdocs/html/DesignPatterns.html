DesignPatterns are a way of describing solutions to common design problems. A typical pattern will detail the problem to be solved and outline a solution in terms of abstract interacting components.

The following are some patterns taken from the two books mentioned at the end of this page. It certainly isn't definitive, even if it is extensive. Some will be useful, some won't, only time will tell...

  • PipesAndFiltersPattern

  • BrokerPattern
