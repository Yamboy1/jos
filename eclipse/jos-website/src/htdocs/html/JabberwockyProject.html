Project Jabberwocky

Synopsis

Jabberwocky will be a GroupWare system based on Java, Web/HTML, and HyperText/HyperMedia principles. The inspiration stems from WikiWikiWeb, which I've just found out to be a huge monster! Increadible.

Goals

JabberwockyProject is intended to replace and surpass WikiWikiWeb, CVS, the MailingLists (and more!), for supporting Web-based FreeWare projects and collaborations, and finally, for integrating into JOS (the OS), for personal/IntraNet applications.
