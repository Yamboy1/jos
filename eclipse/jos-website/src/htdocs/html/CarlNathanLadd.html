Hello, my name is Carl Nathan Ladd (please, call me Nathan). I've been following the JOS project for a short time now, and am very interested in the UserInterfaceGroup and the GameGroup working groups.

I'm 20 years old, a Microsoft Certified Product Specialist, and a consultant for the Scott Consulting Corporation in Eagan, MN. In the past 5 1/2 years I've worked with several companies, primarily doing system/network administration and application development. I've had the chance to work with over a dozen different operating systems (the MS family of OS'es, Ultrix, the BSD family, Solaris 2.6, AIX, DG-UX, HP-UX, OS/2 (2.x to 4), and VMS) - each of which has a slightly different user interface.

In working with all of those systems, I have yet to find a UI that I'm comfortable using. Each and every one has a feature missing that I would consider essential. So, of course, I jumped at the chance to be a part of this project, especially the UserInterfaceGroup.

This looks to be a great project, with even greater potential. I'm glad to be a part of it, and I look forward to working with everyone involved. :)

-- CarlNathanLadd
